Event Types

Conquest

Big ass maps where you need to hold points like domination in call of duty but civil war ya know?

Skirmish

Normal battle, Officer, NCOs, flag, and maps dependent on event cord. Live on restart. No ramboing. No line or move out restrictions.

Picket Patrol

Five rounds side with the most casualties loses. Last team standing wins. One life each round. You will die if you are out of line. No garrison buildings, sharps, cav, nco, or flag.
